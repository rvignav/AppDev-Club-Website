Discover New Content

Each club meeting is dedicated to a new app dev topic, from frameworks such as React and Flutter to languages such as Swift and HTML. Our goal is to share diverse and valuable curriculum to all our members; we start out with presentations on various software topics, then hone in on specific skills by applying that content to group projects. Join the club to take part in the fun!

Projects & Competitions

Not only do we apply each session's content by engaging in group projects, but we also provide members with the tools needed to attend coding competitions, such as hackathons. Our App Dev Club members have won LA Hacks (Southern California's largest hackathon) two years in a row, and we have represented Saratoga by placing at various other hackathons.

Path to the Future

App Dev Club's underlying purpose is to provide its students with the skills needed to pursue a career in software and app development. With the fundamental programming knowledge as well as the coding tips and tricks you gain from App Dev Club, you're on your way to making your future brighter.
